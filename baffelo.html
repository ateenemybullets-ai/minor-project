The Resilience of Buffalo Farming in Many Zones

Buffalo are large, powerful bovines vital to agriculture in Asia and elsewhere, known for milk (River buffalo) or draft power (Swamp buffalo), with two main types (River & Swamp) originating from the Asian wild buffalo, valued for milk, meat, hide, and strength, and also featuring distinct wild relatives like the African Cape buffalo and American bison, showcasing adaptability but requiring wallowing to cool off.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the main difference between an Arid and Tropical guide? +

Arid guides focus on **water efficiency, drought-tolerant breeds, and temperature management** during extreme heat. Tropical guides prioritize **disease and parasite control** due to humidity, and housing to promote air flow.
